UW-NLP Discourse Reading Group

Introduction

Welcome to the UW-NLP Discourse Reading Group!
During the 2017 winter quarter, we will read about discourse – modeling the semantic relation between sentences. We will study different aspects of discourse, including how discourse information can benefit downstream tasks, such as text summarization and machine translation.

Logistics

Our meetings will take place on Mondays, 3-4pm, at CSE 303.
